I'm Naren, a junior majoring in Mechanical Engineering at Arizona State University. This website showcases a team project I worked on through the EPICS program, where our group of seven developed 3D tactile maps to support visually impaired individuals. Our goal is to design and create a touch-based 3D map of the ASU Tempe campus that is accessible, easy to understand, and helpful for navigation.

Project Overview

The 3D Tactile Maps Project focuses on creating accessible, touch-friendly maps designed for individuals with visual impairments. By converting 2D campus layouts and topographic images into detailed, 3D printable models, these tactile maps help users understand and navigate spaces through touch.
